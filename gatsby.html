Tasty and crispy cheese gasby

Meant to be shared among several people, the Gatsby consists of a foot-long baguette that is sliced open and filled with various ingredients, the only constants being either French fries or slap chips and hot sauce, either achar (typically mango pickle) or piri-piri.
